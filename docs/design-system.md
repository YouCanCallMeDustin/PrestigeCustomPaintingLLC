# Template Direction: Modern Conversion (Contractors)

**Intended For:** Aggressive growth contractors, Exterior remodelers (Siding/Windows), Landscapers, Painters
**Core Goal:** Maximize digital lead generation, capture user intent quickly, and present a sharp, modern, tech-forward image.

---

## 1. Homepage Layout & Section Order
1. **Header:** Sticky, minimal padding, simple navigation. Prominent "Get a Quote" button that triggers a modal wizard.
2. **Hero Section:** Dynamic (subtle video background or high-end slider). Big, punchy headline. An interactive multi-step lead capture form is embedded *directly* into the right side of the hero.
3. **Social Proof / "As Seen In":** Greyscale logos of commercial clients, or supplier badges (e.g., "James Hardie Preferred", "Sherwin Williams").
4. **How It Works (Process):** 3 simple steps emphasizing ease. (1. Request Quote -> 2. We Do the Work -> 3. You Enjoy).
5. **Core Services:** Interactive cards with hover effects (image zooms, overlay text appears).
6. **Before & After Gallery:** Interactive image sliders (drag to see before/after) to visually prove competency.
7. **Value Props (Icon Grid):** Fast turnaround, Financing available, Eco-friendly materials.
8. **Final CTA:** Large bold block: "Ready to transform your property? Get your instant quote."
9. **Footer:** Minimalist footer. Focus on essential links, social media, and a final newsletter/lead gen input.

## 2. Visual Personality
- **Vibe:** Crisp, active, tech-forward, efficient, premium but accessible.
- **Layout:** Sharper edges, slight drop shadows, interactive hover states, compartmentalized grid systems.

## 3. Color Palette Direction
- **Primary:** Vibrant Electric Blue or Energetic Emerald Green.
- **Secondary (Action):** High-contrast Yellow or Bright Magenta for conversion buttons.
- **Backgrounds:** Light Gray, offset with dark Charcoal or almost-black sections for high contrast.

## 4. Recommended Fonts
- **Headings:** `Inter` or `Poppins` — modern, clean, geometric sans-serif.
- **Body:** `Inter` — excellent web readability.

## 5. CTA Language
- "Get My Instant Quote"
- "See Pricing"
- "Book Online"
- "Start Your Project"

## 6. Trust Sections
- **Live Notifications:** (Optional but effective) "John from Spokane just booked an estimate!" popup.
- **Financing Badges:** Logos of financing partners (e.g., GreenSky, Synchrony) to reduce friction.
- **Before/After:** The strongest trust signal for this tier is visual proof of transformation.

## 7. Local SEO Elements
- **Dynamic Text Replacement:** If running Google Ads, the H1 dynamically changes to match the user's search (e.g., "Siding Contractors in [City]").
- **Service Area Pages:** Footer links to dedicated landing pages for specific suburbs.
- **Fast Speed:** Code optimization for fast loading is crucial for conversion rates.

## 8. Mobile-First Behavior
- **Multi-step Form Wizard:** Large, finger-friendly tap targets. Breaking the form into multiple steps (e.g., "What service do you need?" -> "Zip Code" -> "Name/Email") drastically improves mobile completion rates.
- **Thumb-Zone Navigation:** Bottom navigation bar for core actions (Home, Services, Quote).

## 9. Recommended Reusable Components
- `MultiStepLeadGenForm`
- `BeforeAfterImageSlider`
- `InteractiveServiceCards`
- `ProcessTimeline`
