## The 9-Step Prestige Painting Process {#9-step-process}

### Step 1: Site Evaluation & Color Consultation
What to do: We assess the substrate condition and help select the perfect palette.
Why it matters: Ensures the color matches your home’s character and regional light.
**Pro tip:** Test samples in both morning and evening light to see the true undertones.

### Step 2: Industrial Power Washing (Exteriors)
What to do: Removing Spokane’s seasonal grime, mold, and loose debris.
Why it matters: Paint only sticks to what it's touching. Clean surfaces are non-negotiable.
**Common pitfall:** Skipping the dry time after washing, which traps moisture under the paint.

### Step 3: Meticulous Masking & Protection
What to do: Covering windows, landscaping, and floors with heavy-duty plastic and drop cloths.
Why it matters: Clean lines are the difference between a DIY look and a "Prestige" finish.

### Step 4: Scraping & Sanding
What to do: Mechanical removal of failing layers.
Why it matters: Prevents "feathering" and ensures a smooth, uniform surface.

### Step 5: High-Performance Priming
What to do: Applying specialized bonding primers from **Sherwin Williams** or **Benjamin Moore**.
Why it matters: Blocks stains and creates a "grip" for the top coat.

### Step 6: Professional Application
What to do: Two coats of premium, high-build paint applied via HVLP spray or expert brush/roll.
**Pro tip:** Using [Benjamin Moore](https://www.benjaminmoore.com/) SCUFF-X for high-traffic interior halls is a game-changer for durability.

### Step 7: Detail Work (Trim & Molding)
What to do: Hand-finishing edges and architectural details.

### Step 8: Total Site Cleanup
What to do: Removing all debris, tape, and equipment. We leave your home cleaner than we found it.

### Step 9: Final Walkthrough with Robert
What to do: A personal inspection to ensure we hit our "Expect Excellence" standard.

## Common Mistakes (and How to Avoid Them)
1. **Painting Over Wet Siding:** Always wait at least 48 hours after rain or power washing.
2. **Ignoring Humidity:** Extreme Spokane dry spells (under 15% humidity) can cause paint to dry too fast, leading to brush marks. We use extenders to maintain a "wet edge."
3. **Using Cheap Caulk:** We use high-grade urethane sealants that stretch with your home during freezing winters.
